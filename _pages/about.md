<span id="handwave">👋🏻</span> Hi! I'm an incoming CS PhD student at [Stanford University](https://www.stanford.edu/), advised by Prof [Fei-Fei Li](https://profiles.stanford.edu/fei-fei-li) as part of the [Stanford Vision and Learning Lab](https://svl.stanford.edu/). Currently, I am also a student researcher working with the [PRIOR](https://prior.allenai.org/) team and the [Robotics](https://allenai.org/embodied-ai/) team at the [Allen Institute for AI](https://allenai.org/).

Previously, I obtained my BS degree from the [University of Washington](https://washington.edu/), where I double majored in computer science (with honors) and statistics, and minored in mathematics. I was privileged to be advised by Prof [Ranjay Krishna](https://www.ranjaykrishna.com/), Prof [Ali Farhadi](https://www.cs.washington.edu/people/faculty/ali-farhadi/), Prof [Dieter Fox](https://homes.cs.washington.edu/~fox/), and Prof [Jenq-Neng Hwang](https://people.ece.uw.edu/hwang/). I was also grateful to be mentored by and collaborate with Prof [Jiafei Duan](https://duanjiafei.com/) and Dr [Ying Jin](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=MNrLDhwAAAAJ&hl=en/).

My research interests lie broadly in robot learning. In particular, I focus on developing foundation models for robotic manipulation that are deployable in the real world and unlock novel capabilities.
